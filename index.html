Travel and See the World, Safely.
We set out to create an app that delivered a safe experience in a health-conscious post-pandemic world. But our research brought the focus to a different kind of safety that people were looking for when planning personal vacations.
Personal Safety.

Shifted Focus. Our initial thought was people needed an app where they can plan trip and know the location is safe from a health perspective. Fresh out of the pandemic we assumed those concerns would still be prevelant. What we found was users were no longer concerned about health related safety but more about personal wellbeing when traveling abroad or to new cities. Through our research we uncovered more than one user more than willing to share a horror story of deceptive photos on an AirBnB listing to a murder scene in a motel parking lot across the street.

Personal Safety is Paramount. In the current travel landscape, safety concerns have become a critical factor for travelers when booking vacations. Despite the availability of numerous travel apps, users often encounter challenges in evaluating the safety of their destinations, accommodations, and transportation options. This gap in information and support leads to increased anxiety, potential safety risks, and a lack of confidence in their travel decisions. Travelers need a solution that provides comprehensive, reliable, and easily accessible safety information, including real-time updates on local safety conditions, emergency contact details, and safety ratings for accommodations and activities. The challenge is to develop a travel app that seamlessly integrates these safety features, ensuring that users can make informed and secure travel choices while enjoying a stress-free vacation planning experience.

We encourage you to see the world, and to travel Safely when you do! Safely is a travel app that prioritizes user safety by offering detailed safety information and resources for destinations, accommodations, and activities. We envisioned an app where we could generate user safety ratings, emergency contact information, and user-friendly interfaces to help travelers make confident and well-informed decisions about their vacation plans.
